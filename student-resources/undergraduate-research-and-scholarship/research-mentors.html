Research Mentors

Contact your research mentor in your department or related field to discuss available research options. Keep in mind, you will be limited to the type of research being conducted by your mentor. 

The College of Sciences offers students a scientific education to meet the needs of the 21st century, allowing them to compete for a variety of careers in an increasingly complex and evolving world.
